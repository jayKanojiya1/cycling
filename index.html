ABOUT US​

At AJ Cycling Classes, we love teaching people how to ride bikes! Whether you're new to cycling or looking to improve your skills, our friendly instructors are here to help. We offer classes for all ages and abilities, so everyone can join in the fun. Plus, we have options for both in-center training and home service, making it easy to fit cycling into your busy schedule.

We believe that cycling is not just a great way to stay active, but also a fun and enjoyable activity for the whole family. Our goal is to make learning to ride a bike easy and stress-free, so you can focus on having a good time. Join us at AJ Cycling Classes and discover the joy of cycling today!

Over the course of five days, our comprehensive cycling program aims to instill confidence and competence in riders of all levels.

Beginning with the basics of mounting, dismounting, and balancing on day one, participants gradually progress to more advanced skills such as steering, turning, and riding on varied terrain.

Each day builds upon the foundation laid in the previous sessions, with a focus on safety, technique, and enjoyment.

By day three, riders are navigating obstacles and practicing basic traffic maneuvers, preparing them for real-world cycling experiences.

Day four introduces advanced techniques like riding with one hand and signaling turns, while day five offers a chance to consolidate learning and celebrate achievements. With expert instruction and supportive guidance, our program equips participants with the skills and confidence to embrace the freedom and joy of cycling.
